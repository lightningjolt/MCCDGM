Welcome to Matthew's Dungeon and Dragon's website!








This website was made originally for me and my friends to share our DND campaign, and it eventually evolved in an entire online community.

Starting off in my friend Jason's basement, we eventually brought our game over to the library, and then online where we average up to 50-100 people a night!

Meet hundreds of other players!

Read up on thousands and thousands of hours worth of lore and games.

Delve into a huge and intricately crafted universe with many different sites to see!


In this site, you will find...

    1. A main story that has been going on for the past Seven years
    2. A community of roleplayers that all enjoy developing their characters and battle fierce monsters!
    3. Meetup spots, and other sub campaigns centered in the magical world of Terminus
    4. A forum dedicated to character development and ideas.

What can you expect from this website?


When first observing this site you may think that this site is made by some nerd in his basement. And you'd be partially right!


However, this site was made over the course of years, starting way back in 2004 and leading into one of the greatest DND websites ever made.


So why don't you go ahead and make click one of those links up at the top, and see what we have in store for you? We promise, it'll totally blow your mind.
